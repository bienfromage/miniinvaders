Here it is! The first beta testing version of MiniInvaders! Since this is the testing version, the controls are not yet described in the game, so I have listed them below:

  • Player 1: arrow keys to move, enter key to shoot
  • Player 2: WASD to move, spacebar to shoot
  • Last player standing wins!
